  • ✝️ Jesus was hurt on a cross for our sins
  • 🪦 Jesus died and was placed in a tomb
  • ⛰️ A big stone was rolled in front of the tomb
  • ☀️ On Sunday morning, Jesus came back to life!
  • ✨ The tomb was EMPTY — He is Risen!
🎨 Today's Craft

✋ "God's Love for Me"

Children traced their hand next to a cross to remember that God's love reaches to THEM personally! Each little hand reminds us that Jesus’ sacrifice is for every child. 💖
